About ProtoDYNA Mobility

Born from curiosity and passion, ProtoDYNA Mobility started as a small team of young engineers experimenting with vehicle dynamics and autonomous systems. What began as a university project quickly evolved into a platform for innovation, learning, and collaboration in electric and autonomous mobility.

We believe that hands-on experience fuels creativity. Every project is a step towards building sustainable, intelligent, and futuristic vehicles that push the boundaries of mobility.
